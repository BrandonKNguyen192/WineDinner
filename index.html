Timothy Klumper

Professional Chef & Food Stylist

Biography

Born in Orange County, California, Tim has dedicated over a decade to the culinary arts. His journey began with formal training through culinary school in Cypress, California. Tim's passion for cooking lies in making connections, nourishing the people he serves, and expressing his artistic side.

Tim's career has seen him master vegetarian and vegan cuisine at Mead's Green Door, delve into authentic Italian dishes at Cucina Enoteca, and explore Japanese, Chinese, and Korean cuisines as a Kitchen Manager for Wolfgang Puck's WP24 at the Ritz-Carlton. He was also part of the opening team for Ardor by John Frasier at the Edition Hotel, where he elevated seasonal vegetables to be the star of the show.

Most recently, Tim has transitioned to the role of Executive Private Chef in Beverly Hills, creating curated meals and memorable experiences for his clients through family dinners, staff meals, and seasonal menus for special events.

The Wine Pairings

2023 Mauritson Sauvignon Blanc, Dry Creek

A crisp and vibrant Sauvignon Blanc from Dry Creek Valley. It presents lively notes of grapefruit, lime, and passionfruit with a subtle hint of lemongrass. Its bright acidity and clean finish make it a perfect match for the delicate Scallop Crudo and the rich "Sweet Corn" Agnolotti.

2022 Charlie Clay Pinot Noir, Sonoma Coast

An elegant and complex Pinot Noir from the cool Sonoma Coast. This wine offers beautiful aromas of red cherry and raspberry, layered with hints of forest floor and spice. Its silky texture and fine-grained tannins gracefully complement the King Salmon without overpowering it.

2022 Rockpile Zinfandel, Rockpile Ridge

A bold and structured Zinfandel from the renowned Rockpile appellation. Expect intense flavors of dark blackberry, plum, and black pepper. Its robust character and firm tannins stand up beautifully to the rich Grilled Australian Lamb Chops, while the wine's fruit notes echo the Amarena cherry jus.
